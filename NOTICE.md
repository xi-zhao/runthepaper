# Notice

RunThePaper publishes our own reproduction notes, code, generated data, generated
figures, checks, and lessons.

It does not redistribute original paper PDFs, arXiv source archives, publisher
source packages, or original paper figure assets.

Some cases were developed with local access to official paper artifacts such as
arXiv source files or published figures. Those artifacts may be referenced in
the reproduction boundary, but they are not part of this repository.

Readers should obtain original papers and source materials from the official
publisher, arXiv, author repository, or benchmark source.
