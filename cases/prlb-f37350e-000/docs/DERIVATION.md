# Derivation

The source density is

$$
\rho_V=9.640\times10^{-5}Y_e^{-1}B_{12}^2E_1^2f^{-2}\;\mathrm{g\,cm^{-3}}.
$$

Changing `B12=100 B14` gives the coefficient `0.9640`. With `f^2=B/(5B_Q)`, Set-B reduces algebraically to `29.313356344 g cm^-3`.

For Fig. 1, in the lossless source approximation,

$$
\beta=\frac{u_e^{1/2}(1-u_i)\sin^2\theta_{kB}}{2\cos\theta_{kB}}
\left(1-\frac{\rho_V}{\rho}\right),\qquad
K_\pm=\beta\pm\sqrt{\beta^2+1}.
$$

The invariant `K_+ K_-=-1` is checked across the plotted density grid.
